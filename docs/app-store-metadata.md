## App Description (Full)
IgnytLIVE is the next-generation live streaming platform where creators connect with their audience in real-time. Broadcast live, watch engaging streams, send virtual gifts, chat with fellow viewers, and build your community.

Key Features:

LIVE STREAMING
- Go live in seconds with one tap
- Watch streams across multiple categories
- Real-time chat with emojis and reactions
- PK battles between streamers

VIRTUAL GIFTING
- Send animated virtual gifts to your favorite creators
- Purchase coins securely via Apple Pay / credit card
- Creators earn diamonds from gifts received

SOCIAL COMMUNITY
- Follow your favorite creators
- Join or create Families (social groups)
- Direct messaging with translation support
- Short-form video sharing

PRIVATE VIDEO CALLS
- 1:1 private video and audio calls
- Per-minute or per-session billing with coins

EVENTS & ENGAGEMENT
- Schedule and RSVP to live events
- Leaderboards for top gifters and creators
- Achievement system with rewards
- Daily login bonuses

SAFETY & MODERATION
- 18+ age verification required
- Real-time chat moderation with mute/ban controls
- User blocking and reporting
- Community guidelines enforcement
