Software Requirements Specification (SRS)Project: LiveStream Connect (Full-Scale Mobile Platform)1. Executive SummaryLiveStream Connect is a high-performance mobile application designed for real-time video broadcasting, social networking, and digital monetization. Inspired by industry leaders like Bigo Live and Clapper, the platform focuses on low-latency streaming, interactive gamification, and a robust creator economy.2. Functional Requirements & Feature ListThe system is divided into three primary modules: Broadcaster, Viewer, and Administrative.2.1 Creator & Broadcaster FeaturesHD Broadcasting: One-to-many video streaming via RTMP/WebRTC protocols.AR Beauty Suite: Real-time facial filters, skin retouching, and 3D stickers.PK Battle Mode: Synchronized split-screen competitive streaming with real-time score tracking based on gift points.Multi-Guest Hosting: Support for "Guest Seats" allowing 1:4 or 1:9 audio/video interaction.Stream Management: In-stream moderation tools (Mute, Kick, Ban) and real-time viewer analytics.2.2 Viewer & Social FeaturesImmersive Viewing: Vertical scroll UI with Picture-in-Picture (PiP) support.Real-time Interaction: Low-latency chat, "Like" bursts, and @mention functionality.Short Video Feed: A TikTok-style algorithm-driven feed for pre-recorded content.Discovery Engine: Search by geolocation (Nearby), trending hashtags, or user levels.Social Graph: Follow/Unfollow mechanics, private DM messaging, and user profiles.2.3 Monetization & GamificationVirtual Gift System: Interactive 2D/3D animations triggered by user-sent gifts.In-App Wallet: Secure storage for "Coins" (purchased) and "Diamonds/Beans" (earned).VIP/Nobility Tiers: Tiered subscription model offering exclusive entry effects, badges, and priority support.Global Leaderboards: Real-time rankings for "Top Gifters" and "Most Popular Streamers" (Daily/Weekly/Monthly).3. Technical Requirements3.1 Non-Functional RequirementsLatency: End-to-end delay must be $< 2.0$ seconds for interactive streams.Scalability: Microservices architecture to support $1M+$ Daily Active Users (DAU).Availability: 99.9% uptime for the streaming API and payment gateway.Security: End-to-end encryption for DMs and PCI-DSS compliance for payment processing.3.2 Recommended Tech StackLayerTechnologyMobile AppFlutter (Cross-platform) or Swift/Kotlin (Native)Video InfrastructureAgora.io or AWS IVS (Streaming, RTC, Recording)BackendNode.js or Go (High-concurrency microservices)Caching/Real-timeRedis (Leaderboards & Live Chat states)DatabasesPostgreSQL (User Data) & MongoDB (Video Metadata)Content DeliveryCloudFront or Cloudflare (CDN)4. System Architecture & Data FlowIngest: The broadcaster's device captures video and pushes it to the Transcoding Server.Processing: The server generates multiple bitrates (Adaptive Bitrate Streaming) to ensure viewers on 3G, 4G, or 5G all have a smooth experience.Distribution: The processed stream is distributed via Edge Nodes (CDN) to minimize physical distance to the viewer.Signal Logic: A WebSocket server handles the real-time chat, gift triggers, and PK score updates simultaneously.5. Administrative & Moderation LogicAI Content Guard: Automated scanning of video frames for prohibited content (Nudity/Violence).Human-in-the-Loop: A dashboard for moderators to review flagged streams and user reports.Payout Management: Administrative portal to approve/process creator withdrawals to external bank accounts or PayPal.6. User Interface (UI) GoalsZero-Friction Entry: One-tap "Go Live" button.Visual Hierarchy: Prominent placement of the "Gift" button and "Top Contributor" list to drive revenue.Community Feel: Vibrant colors, leveling badges, and animated entrance notifications for high-value users.
